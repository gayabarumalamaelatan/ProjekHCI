Sea Reptiles

Sea Reptiles are reptiles wich is lives in a marine environtment and secondarily adapted for being a aquatic or semiaquatic animals. The earliest sea reptile is mesosaurus, arose in the Permian period during the Palezoic era. Many groups of reptiles adapted to live in the seas in the Mesozoic era. Currentl, of the approximaely 12. 000 extant reptile species and subspecies, only about 100 are classed as sea reptiles. The extant sea reptiles are such as sea snakes, sea turtles, saltwater crocodiles, and marine iguanas.
