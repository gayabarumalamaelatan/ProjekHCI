Sea Mammals

Sea mammals are mammals animal that lives in the ocean or other marine ecosystem. Mammals are an informal group, inufied only by their reliance on marine environtments for survival and feeding. The adaptation of marine mammals to an aquatic lifestyle varies considerably between species. sirenians and cetaceans are both fully aquatic and the semiaquatics mammals for example are sea-lions and seals. The semiaquatic mammals spend their time mostly in the water but sometimes they return to land for some inportant activities such as breedingm mating, and molting.
