Crustaceans

Crustaceans are a very diverse group of invertebrate animals wich includes some animals such as lobsters, crabs, shrimp, etc. The crustacean group can be treated as a subphylum under the clade mandibulata. It's now well accepted that the hexapods emerged deep in the crustacean group, with the completed group reffered to as Pancrustaceans. Crustaceans have an exoskeleton, which they moult to grow. Most of the crustaceans are free-living aquatic animals, but some of them are terrestrial, parasitic, and some of them are sessile.
